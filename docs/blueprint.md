# **App Name**: NutriAR

## Core Features:

- Personalized Nutrition Tracking: Log meals, track macros, and receive real-time feedback on nutritional intake based on user goals.
- AI-Powered AR Ingredient Highlighting: Use AR enhanced with AI to identify and highlight ingredients as they are needed in a recipe, supporting visual cooking and providing real-time nutritional info.
- AI-Enhanced AR Cooking Guidance: Overlay step-by-step AR instructions on the user's kitchen environment, enhanced with AI for adaptive guidance on cooking techniques such as chopping, saut√©ing, or plating.
- Intelligent Recipe Suggestions: Suggest recipes based on logged meals, nutritional goals, and user preferences; utilize a tool which determines the suitability of different recipes based on nutritional information and user's health goals.
- Interactive Meal Planning: Create meal plans with integrated AR cooking sessions to visualize each step of the cooking process.
- Community Recipe Sharing: Enable users to share AR cooking experiences and recipes, providing inspiration and support.
- AI Cooking Assistant: Provide real-time AI assistance during AR cooking sessions, answering questions and offering tips as the user cooks.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: White (#FFFFFF) for a clean, modern feel.
- Background color: Light khaki (#F0E68C) for a warm, neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted green (#A9BC82) to highlight important actions and nutritional info, evoking health and nature subtly.
- Body and headline font: 'Open Sans', a humanist sans-serif with a modern, approachable look, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clear, modern icons representing food groups, cooking steps, and nutritional information. Adopt a minimalist style.
- Design a clean, intuitive layout with clear navigation and easily accessible features, optimizing for AR integration. Focus on user-friendly design.
- Incorporate smooth transitions and subtle animations to guide users through the app and enhance the AR experience. Keep animations lightweight and purposeful.
